Overview of the Grammar Topic:

Comparison of adjectives happens in three forms.: Positive, Comparative and Superlative. We can only consider the comparison of only quality while using the Comparative or Superlative adjectives. There are mainly six ways in which different adjectives form their comparatives and superlatives. We need to use the term ‘than’ while using the comparative degree adjectives.
